About My Art

I work in a number of different mediums, ranging from acrylic and water-color painting, epoxy resin, photography, graphite, and practically anything else I can get my hands on. In every piece I make, I try to make all of them unique, fun, and vibrant by experimenting with new techniques.

I hope you enjoy my work as much as I enjoy creating it! Here's a variety of my work for you to enjoy, as well as a link to my shop and commissions if you're interested in purchasing any of my pieces.

About Me

I have always been passionate about art and creativity, and I love using my talents to bring joy to others. I found resin art during a difficult period of my life, and it quickly became not only one of my favorite mediums but also a form of therapy for me. I hope you enjoy my work as much as I enjoy creating it!
